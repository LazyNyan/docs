## YouTube Music (Pear)
Make sure u download [pear desktop](https://github.com/pear-devs/pear-desktop/)

go to plugins at the top, enable `API Server`. 

all of the default settings should be fine. u can mess with the other plugins later..

*jingle jingle*

**on the music overlay:** click YouTube music

:::caution[Important!]
 **MAKE SURE YOU PRESS ALLOW IF YOUR BROWSER ASKS.** This will let the website connect to apps on your computer (specifically yt in this case)
![permission prompt](../../../assets/images/aod.png)
:::

Leave everything as default unless u changed it (if you have to ask, no you didnt)
if it doesnt auto populate enter what you see in the placeholders:

- Connection Address: `127.0.0.1`
- Port: `26538`
- Access Token: Leave blank

Press authorize. If you have confirmations on you need to press allow in the pear app.

YAYYY you're done! Now you can customize it by clicking the brush tab on the left hand side of the sidebar

## Spotify (Premium required)

Go to the [Spotify Developer Dashboard](https://developer.spotify.com/dashboard) and sign in

Click "Create App" and fill in:

- App name: Nyan Music Overlay
- App description: Anything you want
- Redirect URI: https://music.nyako.cat/setup
- Which API/SDKs are you planning to use? Select "Web API"

Click "Save".

Copy your credentials

On your app's settings page, copy the Client ID and Client Secret (click "View client secret" to reveal it)

on the widget select Spotify and paste your Client ID and Client Secret.

hit authorize

done

## Spotify for FREE?!?!??!

Then follow the [Last.fm](#lastfm) steps.
